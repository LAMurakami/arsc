SLM HSM_Copy scheme

The SLM HSM_Copy scheme is one of the user accessible schemes. See SgetS for a complete list.

The SLM HSM_Copy scheme is created & maintained automatically by the Ssync daemon.

The SLM HSM_Copy scheme has attributes related to the Tape and DR copies including Copy_Number and Media_Type.
